Sláinte (slahn-chuh): Irishspeak for cheers
Sláinte is a 5-man Irish band, based out of Boston. Formed in a dorm room at Boston College, the boys from The Heights have been playing pubs and parties in the Boston area since 2013. With upbeat sets featuring a wide range of tunes from Irish classics to Dropkick Murphys covers, and everything in between, Sláinte always brings the craic!
